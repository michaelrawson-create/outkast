OutKast's Impact on Hip Hop

OutKast revolutionized hip hop by bringing Southern flavor and innovation to a genre dominated by East and West Coast sounds. Their eclectic production, genre-bending albums, and creative lyricism helped redefine what hip hop could be, paving the way for future Southern artists and expanding the boundaries of mainstream rap. They are widely considered the greatest hip hop group of all time.
